#### Ingredients

## Foods

### Food no 1  

#### Ingredients

a> Tin of baked beans
b> An egg/or, pair of egg(s)
c> Rice 

#### Instructions

1> Get a frying pan
2> Pour into the frying pan...the tin of baked beans
3> Also, pour in the egg/s
4> When everything is ready/cooked...
5> Cook some rice
6> Eat the baked beans, and, eggs...with the rice
